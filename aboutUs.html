About Out Modern

Welcome to Out Modern, your best choice source for all fashion outfit. Out Modern have the latest fashion outfits and get ready to giving you the best product, with focus on customer demand.


Founded in 2003 by Yap Zi Yan, Out Modern has come a long way from its beginnings in a small shop lots in Kuala Lumpur. When he first started out, his passion for making the outfit for his family and drove him to do In-depth research about costume design and gave him the motive force to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the fashion industry.


We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments to ask, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are Simple, Happy and Together. These key words are visible at every step of the Out Modern journey.

Our Purpose

To expand our business to create more sales and maximize our sales internationally by providing a platform to connect buyers and sellers within one community.

Our Positioning

To connect users across the region, Out Modern offers a user-friendly online shopping experience that provides a wide selection of best products and services.

Our Personality

To introduce who we are and how we talk, behave to any given situation as customers are always right.

Simple

We make a simplicity and integrity of life that’s honest and true to self.

Happy

We are friendly, fun-loving and bursting with joyful energy with everyone we meet.

Together

We enjoy spending quality time with you while shopping online and doing the things we love.
